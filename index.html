About Us

Byrne Accident Investigations was established in 1999 and has grown to be at the forefront of claims investigations.
We work on behalf of Insurance companies and the MIBI to provide a nationwide service tailored to the requirements of our valued clients.
We specialise in Motor, Employers Liability and Public Liability claims investigation.
Our investigative staff include CIP qualified personnel with over forty years claims experience and a solicitor. We also have a full administrative staff in our offices.
We use the latest technology and case management system to ensure our IT systems are fully secure to enable our investigators on the road to have safe remote access.
Byrne Accident Investigations pride ourselves on providing a high level of customer service and prompt and thorough investigation.
